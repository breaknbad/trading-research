## 🎯 Pitch Template

### Cold Call Script (30 seconds)

> "Hi, this is [Name] with Mi AI. We help local businesses in [town] get found online. I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website yet — and right now, customers searching for [service] in [town] are finding your competitors instead.
>
> We build professional websites for local businesses starting at $500, and we can have yours live in under a week. Can I show you a quick demo of what yours could look like?"

### Follow-Up Email / Text

**Subject:** Your customers are searching — are they finding you?

>

>

> **[Demo Link]**

>
> Our packages start at $500 with no long-term contracts. Can I give you a quick 5-minute call this week?
>
> Best,
> [Name] | Mi AI

### In-Person Drop-In (for local businesses)

1. Walk in, introduce yourself
2. Pull up the demo on your phone: "I actually mocked up what your site could look like"
3. Show it — let them react
4. Leave a one-page flyer with pricing + QR code to the demo
5. Follow up in 2-3 days
