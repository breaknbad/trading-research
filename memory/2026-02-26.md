## Lessons
- Don't force longs in risk-off tape ‚Äî inverse ETFs exist
- Cash is a position but cash hoarding kills returns ‚Äî min 50% deployed by 10:00
- Tighter stops (1.5%) enable higher deployment with same risk
- Max portfolio heat 6% ($1,500 total stop exposure)
- Volume-before-price = early scout signal (institutional accumulation)
- **Code > vibes** ‚Äî 4 bots writing essays about the same NVDA drop for an hour instead of one script that trades it. Mark was right to demand code.
- **Free APIs require graceful degradation** ‚Äî don't let missing volume data block all trades. Downgrade tier instead of blocking.
- **DB field casing matters** ‚Äî `OPEN` vs `open` caused zero positions to load
